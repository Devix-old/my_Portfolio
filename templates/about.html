About Us

How did this idea come to me? I was looking for an idea for a website to work on and benefit from at the same time. Sitting with my mother, as we were approaching Ramadan—a month of significant cooking—it struck me: why not create a website where I write about a dish, and it gives me the recipe to cook it. Delving into programming the website, I realized it was a fantastic idea. Now, I have my own site that teaches me how to cook, intertwining technology, intimacy, and gastronomy.
